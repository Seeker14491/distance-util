Steam IDs of Founding Modders.

Founding Modders are people who backed Distance on Kickstarter at the $500 tier or higher. The IDs were extracted from game code. Note these are not all the Founding Modders; the Kickstarter page shows 17 people backing at the $500 tier and higher, while this list is much shorter.
